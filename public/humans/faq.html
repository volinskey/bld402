Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for anyone curious about bld402.

What is bld402?

bld402 is a free service that helps you build and deploy web applications without writing any code. You describe what you want to an AI agent (like Claude Code, ChatGPT, or Cursor), point it to bld402.com, and the agent builds and deploys a working app for you — usually in under 10 minutes.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. That's the whole point. You describe your idea in plain English, and your AI agent handles all the technical work. bld402 provides the agent with templates, step-by-step instructions, and best practices so it can build a real, working application.

How much does it cost?

bld402 itself is completely free. The apps you build run on run402 infrastructure, where prototype projects start at $0.10 (enough for 7 days). Your AI agent handles the payment automatically using cryptocurrency (USDC), or you can set up a Stripe subscription.

What kinds of things can I build?

Anything you can describe: todo lists, voting apps, trivia games, landing pages with email signups, expense trackers, recipe books, workout logs, flash card apps, and more. Check out the Showcase for examples and the Templates for ready-made starting points.

How long do my apps last?

It depends on the plan your agent chooses. Prototype projects last 7 days. Hobby projects last 30 days and can be renewed. After a lease expires, your app goes read-only for 7 days, then gets archived, and is permanently deleted after 37 days. You can renew anytime before deletion.

Which AI agent should I use?

Any coding agent works: Claude Code, ChatGPT (with code execution), Cursor, Windsurf, Cline, or others. Just tell it what you want and include "go to bld402.com" in your prompt. The agent will read the instructions and handle everything.

What if my app has a bug?

Tell your AI agent what's wrong. It can read the deployed code, diagnose the issue, and redeploy a fix. You can also ask it to add features or change the design after the initial build.

Can I edit my app after it's deployed?

Yes. Just tell your AI agent what you want to change. It can update the code and redeploy. You can iterate as many times as you want within your project's lease period.

Is my data private?

Your app's data is stored on run402 infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Each project is isolated in its own database schema. See run402's Privacy Policy for full details.

Who can see my app?

Your deployed app gets a public URL (like yourapp.sites.run402.com). Anyone with the link can visit it. If your app has authentication (login/signup), you control who can access what.

What happens to apps that violate the rules?

Apps deployed through bld402 must follow run402's Acceptable Use Policy. Apps containing malware, phishing, illegal content, or other prohibited material can be taken down without notice. You're responsible for everything your AI agent deploys on your behalf.

I don't need templates. I can code — I just need infrastructure.

You want run402 — it's the infrastructure layer that powers bld402. Run402 gives you direct access to Postgres, REST APIs, auth, storage, and static site hosting. Paste https://run402.com/llms.txt into your agent's chat and you're ready to go.
