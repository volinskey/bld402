Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-05

Overview

bld402 is a stateless website. It does not collect, store, or process any personal data. There are no user accounts, no cookies, no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no server-side logging of visitor activity. When you visit bld402.com, you are reading static HTML pages — nothing more.

Data We Collect

None. bld402 does not collect any personal information, usage data, or device information. We do not use cookies, local storage, or any other client-side tracking mechanism.

Infrastructure and Hosting

Apps built through bld402 run on run402 infrastructure. Any data you create in your apps — database records, uploaded files, user accounts — is stored and processed by run402 and governed by run402's privacy policy. bld402 does not have access to this data.

bld402.com itself is hosted using standard web hosting services. These services may collect standard server access logs (IP address, request timestamp, page requested) as part of normal operations. bld402 does not access, review, or use these logs.

AI Agent Interactions

During the build process, your AI agent may store project information (such as API credentials and your app description) in its own memory. This data is managed entirely by your AI agent's platform under its own privacy policy. bld402 does not transmit data to, receive data from, or otherwise interact with your AI agent's platform.

Third-Party Services

bld402 relies on run402 for backend infrastructure (databases, APIs, hosting) provided to apps built through the bld402 workflow. run402 may use sub-processors and third-party services to deliver its infrastructure. Details are available in run402's privacy policy and sub-processor documentation.

bld402 uses Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect standard web request data (IP address) when fonts are loaded. See Google's privacy policy for details.

Children's Privacy

bld402 is not directed at children under 13. Because bld402 collects no data, there is no risk of collecting children's personal information. However, apps built through bld402 and hosted on run402 are the responsibility of their creators with respect to age-appropriate content and data collection.

International Data Transfers

bld402 itself does not transfer any data internationally because it does not collect any data. For data stored by apps built through bld402, run402's privacy policy governs applicable data transfer mechanisms.

Your Rights

Because bld402 stores no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or port. If you have questions about data stored by an app built through bld402, refer to run402's privacy policy for data subject rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of bld402 after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Privacy questions? Reach out via the run402 contact channels.
