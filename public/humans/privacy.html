Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-04

bld402 Stores Nothing

bld402 is a stateless service. It does not store any user data, session information, cookies, analytics, or personal information. There are no accounts, no login, and no tracking.

What bld402 Is

bld402 is a static website that provides instructions, templates, and workflows for AI agents. When you visit bld402.com, you are reading HTML pages. No data is collected or transmitted to bld402.

What run402 Stores

Apps built through bld402 use run402 for infrastructure. Data you create in your apps (database records, uploaded files, user accounts) is stored by run402 and governed by run402's privacy policy.

AI Agent Memory

During the build process, your AI agent stores project information (like API keys and your app description) in its own memory. This data is managed by your AI agent's platform (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) under their respective privacy policies. bld402 does not access or store this information.

Third-Party Services

  • run402.com — Backend infrastructure for apps you build
  • AWS CloudFront/S3 — Hosts deployed apps
  • AWS Amplify — Hosts bld402.com itself
